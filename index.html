eve.ai

Hiring Without Hassle

eve.ai is an AI-based system that can shortlist resumes based on a given job description. eve.ai is a cross-platform cloud-based SaaS product which analyses your resume and ranks it accordingly based on our unique and tested algorithms.

Features

We bring you a lot of unique features

Neural Networks

eve.ai employs advanced neural networks and natural language processing for analysing every resume. Score generation is based on multiple parameters and hence its more reliable.

Resume Summarizer

eve.ai generates a precise and concise summary of every resume so that the HR doesn't have to read through the entire resume.

Fully conversational Chatbot

Slack has been a medium of communication lately. Eve bot enables HR's to view and shortlist candidates by using a chat interface.

Mailing system

Our product has an in-built mailing system which will notify the selected applicants.
